A Winter to Winter Thing

A wise old man, who thought he knew
All the world's this and that,
Sat next to a young lad on a park bench,
Thinking he'd baffle him with a question up his hat.
So the old man asked the boy afresh,
"Tell me son, what is love?"
The lad, snoozing nice and relaxed,
Cleared his throat and looked at the sky above.
"Love is the sky, it changes color
According to how it feels,
Black during a storm, blue when it's clear,
Because a mundane sky is something nobody needs.
Love is the tree that sways in the wind,
The wind of flowing emotion,
It sways and sways but stays it's place
Even during the worst of season.
From seasons, I recall,
People say love is the summer- bright and shining,
But oh dear chap, I'd say otherwise
I'd say it's a winter to winter thing..."

"Winter?!", the man exclaimed
"But winter's so cruel and cold!"
"Well", the boy smiled and said
"How else can you know what love can uphold?
In summer, the days are happy and easy
The world is free to roam,
The flowers are blooming, the skies are shining,
Everyone is outside their home.
In winter, you see, it's bitter and dark
It stings, it bites and it tests your power
But it is truly only in winter,
That you're warm at home, in the darkest hour.
Love is the rain that falls on rocks,
It washes away the soil,
Leaves you feeling fresh and new
Free and renewed from your turmoil.
Love is the snow that covers the ground,
Leaves a blanket so white and joyful,
But be warned! The same snow can melt,
And make you trip if you're not careful.
Love is the pain you feel in your heart
The purest, most helpless form of sorrow,
It's the tears that just don't stop
Until you've fallen asleep on your pillow.
Love is the joy, the smile and laugh
You see on an infant's face-
Raw, truthful, pristine, divine
Enough to fill all your days.
Love is devotion, love is faith,
Love is the thing keeping you sane,
It can conquer, it can overcome anything
As long as keep it alive."

The old man, stuck in awe had nothing to say,
Just a tear rolling down his cheek,
For on that warm summer day in a garden,
A young lad left him feeling weak.

The Cure

Far away, in this moonlight
Are you thinking of me?
In your head, in your mind?
Tell me now, don't keep this from me
I can't take it anymore
I'm waiting..

Waiting for an answer,
A solution I can't find
Waiting for a single word
That leaves the pain behind
Waiting for the sunshine
A spark of dawn that's new
Waiting for a cure
A cure that's called you.
